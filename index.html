🌈 The 9 Auspicious Days

Day 1 - Shailputri

Color: Red | She is the Daughter of the Himalayas, symbol of strength and purity.

Day 2 - Brahmacharini

Color: Blue | Symbolizes wisdom, penance, and devotion.

Day 3 - Chandraghanta

Color: Yellow | Warrior goddess who blesses devotees with courage and bravery.

Day 4 - Kushmanda

Color: Green | Creator of the universe with her cosmic energy.

Day 5 - Skandamata

Color: Grey | Mother of Lord Kartikeya, symbol of motherhood and compassion.

Day 6 - Katyayani

Color: Orange | Fierce warrior form, remover of sins and obstacles.

Day 7 - Kalaratri

Color: White | Fierce form who destroys ignorance and removes fears.

Day 8 - Mahagauri

Color: Purple | Goddess of purity and serenity, represents peace and wisdom.

Day 9 - Siddhidatri

Color: Peacock Green | Bestower of supernatural powers and ultimate blessings.
